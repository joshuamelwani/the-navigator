The LOST Protocol

Use this reset when you feel spiritually scattered.

👂 L — Listen Pause. Open Scripture. Sit in silence before doing anything.
🪞 O — Own Admit where your mind or actions have drifted. No shame. Just honesty.
🔄 S — Switch Pick one mindset model and return to it. Don’t overthink.
🗣️ T — Tell Reach out. Say it out loud. Don’t isolate.
