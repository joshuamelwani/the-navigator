L.O.S.T. Protocol

L - Look Up
Pray. Ask God to recenter you in truth.
O - Observe
What’s happening in your body, thoughts, and environment?
S - Speak
Say what’s true. Declare what’s promised. Confess what’s not of God.
T - Take One Step
Obey one small thing you feel led to do. Forward movement restores clarity.
