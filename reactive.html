When life hits, look here.

10 Biblical mental models for pressure, uncertainty, and decision.

Joseph Frame

[Think]

You feel stuck or sidelined.

📖 Genesis 50:20
→ This delay may be preparation, not punishment.

Time vs Timing

[Think]

You’re rushing outcomes.

📖 Ecclesiastes 3:1
→ Let God set the pace.

Stewardship

[Think]

You’re overwhelmed by responsibility.

📖 Luke 16:10
→ You manage what God owns. It’s not all on you.

Mirror First

[Think]

You’re judging or comparing others.

📖 James 1:23–25
→ Check yourself before correcting someone else.

Narrow Path

[Do]

Everyone’s doing it — should you?

📖 Matthew 7:13–14
→ Follow conviction, not the crowd.

Return to Others

[Do]

You’ve pulled away from people.

📖 1 Corinthians 12:12
→ Don’t isolate. Reach out.

Pause Response

[Do]

You’re about to react emotionally.

📖 Proverbs 19:2
→ Stop. Breathe. Respond with wisdom.

Identity Check

[Think]

You feel insecure or unworthy.

📖 Romans 8:16
→ You’re already accepted — act from that.

Spiritual Reset

[Do]

You feel dry, disconnected, distracted.

📖 John 15:5
→ Reconnect before continuing.

Plant Anyway

[Do]

You feel unseen or ineffective.

📖 Galatians 6:9
→ Keep going. God sees your faithfulness.
